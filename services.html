Bridge Engineering

Bridge Design & Rehabilitation

With 20+ years of experience in all phases of highway and railroad bridge engineering, Polaris provides design and analysis support for new bridges, widenings, and complex rehabilitation projects. Our work covers steel, prestressed concrete, and cast-in-place concrete structures.

  • New bridge design — steel plate girders, PSC girders, box culverts, rigid frame culverts
  • Bridge widening and stage construction engineering
  • Rehabilitation design — deck replacement, bearing replacement, joint replacement
  • Steel section retrofit, painting, concrete patching and crack repair
  • Abutment, pier, footing, and pile foundation design
  • Substructure design — wall-type abutments, cap & column piers, hammerhead piers
  • Curved and skewed bridge analysis (MIDAS, CSI Bridge, MDX, DESCUS)
  • Shop drawing review and construction phase engineering support

Inspection & Load Rating

NBIS Bridge Inspection & Load Rating

Certified FHWA NBIS Safety Inspector with extensive field inspection and load rating experience across state DOT and transit agency bridge inventories. Polaris provides thorough inspection programs with clear, well-documented deliverables.

  • NBIS bridge safety inspections — steel, concrete, timber, and culvert structures
  • Routine, fracture-critical, and underwater bridge inspection
  • LRFR and LFR load rating analyses per AASHTO MBE
  • Load rating reports and documentation (AASHTO BrR software)
  • Bridge condition assessment and prioritization studies
  • As-built load rating for new construction projects
  • AREMA load rating for railroad bridge structures
  • Posting evaluation and overweight vehicle permit review

Structural Engineering

Structural & Geotechnical Design

Beyond bridges, Polaris provides structural engineering support for transportation-related infrastructure, commercial buildings, and site structures. All designs are code-compliant with ASCE-7, ACI, AISC, and IBC standards.

  • Retaining wall design — cantilever, MSE, soldier pile with lagging, sheet piles
  • Commercial rooftop RTU / equipment support framing
  • Building structural modifications and tenant improvements
  • Foundation design — drilled shafts, driven piles, spread footings
  • Highway sign structure design and mast arm signal structures
  • Noise wall design and replacement
  • Parking structure inspection and repair design
  • Airport facility structural inspections

Construction Engineering

Shoring, Jacking & Construction Support

Polaris provides specialized temporary works design and construction engineering support — helping contractors safely execute complex staged construction operations on bridge and infrastructure projects.

  • Temporary shoring design — up to 30+ feet retained height
  • Bridge jacking and blocking plans for bearing replacement
  • Staged construction engineering and erection analysis
  • Temporary support towers and falsework design
  • MIDAS bridge erection stage analysis
  • Jumper span design for temporary track shifts
  • Constructability reviews and value engineering
  • QC/QA plan development and construction plan review

Building Engineering

Commercial Building Design & Engineering Evaluation

Beyond transportation infrastructure, Polaris provides structural engineering design and evaluation services for commercial buildings and facilities. With a background spanning transit stations, airport terminals, and parking structures, we bring a rigorous, code-compliant approach to commercial building challenges.

  • Structural evaluation and condition assessment of existing commercial buildings
  • Steel framing design — beams, columns, connections per AISC
  • Rooftop RTU and mechanical equipment support framing design
  • Steel joist and roof deck capacity checks and upgrade design
  • Structural modification design for tenant improvements and renovations
  • Foundation evaluation, underpinning, and repair design
  • Building structural inspection and engineering reports
  • Parking garage structural inspection and repair engineering
