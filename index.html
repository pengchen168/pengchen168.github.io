ABOUT THE FIRM

Professional Engineering Excellence

Polaris Bridge Engineering LLC is a civil and structural engineering consulting firm specializing in practical, code-compliant, and construction-ready engineering solutions for transportation, infrastructure and building projects. The firm is committed to delivering high-quality technical services that prioritize safety, constructability, and long-term structural performance.

Our core expertise includes bridge design, bridge rehabilitation, bridge load rating, and bridge inspections, as well as parking structures, transit facilities, commercial and residential buildings, retaining walls, culverts, foundations, and temporary structural support solutions. We provide responsive and detail-oriented engineering support tailored to the needs of public agencies, consultants, and contractors.

Founded by a licensed Professional Engineer with extensive experience in public infrastructure and structural engineering, Polaris Bridge Engineering LLC performs all work in accordance with AASHTO, AREMA, ACI, ASCE 7, AISC, IBC, and applicable State and Local Authority design standards. The firm brings strong agency knowledge and a thorough understanding of transportation and building project requirements.

As a Certified Virginia DBE/MBE firm, Polaris Bridge Engineering LLC is actively seeking opportunities to partner with prime consultants and contractors as a subcontractor on Federal, State, and local projects. We are dedicated to providing reliable technical support, competitive service, and high-quality deliverables to help project teams meet schedule, budget, and regulatory requirements.
