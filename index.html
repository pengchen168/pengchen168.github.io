ABOUT THE FIRM

Professional Engineering Excellence

Polaris Bridge Engineering LLC is a civil and structural engineering consulting firm delivering practical, code-compliant, and construction-ready solutions for transportation and building infrastructure projects.

Our expertise includes bridge design, rehabilitation, load rating, and inspections, as well as parking structures, transit facilities, commercial and residential buildings, retaining walls, culverts, foundations, temporary support systems, and structural retrofit projects for both bridges and buildings.

Founded by a licensed Professional Engineer with extensive public infrastructure experience, the firm provides high-quality engineering services in accordance with AASHTO, AREMA, ACI, ASCE 7, AISC, IBC, and applicable state and local standards.

As a certified Virginia DBE/MBE firm, Polaris Bridge Engineering LLC supports state and federally funded transportation projects and welcomes partnerships with prime consultants and contractors.
