Career Summary

20 Years of Bridge & Infrastructure Engineering

Peng Chen is a senior civil structural engineer and principal of Polaris Bridge Engineering LLC, with 20 years of experience in all phases of highway bridge, railroad bridge, subway station, and transportation infrastructure design and project management.

He is proficient in AASHTO bridge design codes, AREMA railroad design standards, ACI 318, IBC, ASCE-7, and AISC steel design, as well as the specific design requirements of VDOT, ODOT, NYDOT, TxDOT, and FDOT. His structural design expertise spans straight and curved steel girder bridges, prestressed concrete girder bridges, reinforced concrete abutments, piers, footings, and pile foundations.

Mr. Chen is also experienced in concrete deck design, concrete slab bridges, precast and rigid frame culverts, highway retaining walls (sheet piles, MSE walls, soldier pile with lagging), highway sign structures, mast arm signal structures, and drilled shaft foundations. As a certified NBIS bridge inspector, he brings extensive field inspection experience across a broad range of bridge types.

Polaris Bridge Engineering LLC operates as a Certified Virginia DBE/MBE firm, actively seeking teaming opportunities with prime consultants and contractors on federal, state, and local infrastructure projects.
