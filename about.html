Principal-Led Bridge and Structural Engineering

Polaris Bridge Engineering LLC is a specialized civil and structural engineering consulting firm focused on bridges, transportation infrastructure, construction engineering, and complex existing-structure assignments.

The firm was founded by Peng Chen, P.E., to give clients direct access to senior technical judgment, practical project delivery, and responsive communication. Polaris supports prime consultants, contractors, public owners, and facility owners through focused scopes, task-order assignments, specialty analysis, independent review, and construction-phase engineering.

Peng Chen, P.E. | Founder and Principal

Peng Chen has more than 20 years of progressive bridge and structural engineering experience spanning design, rehabilitation, load rating, inspection, construction engineering, and owner-side technical review. His work has involved major highway, railroad, transit, airport, and facility programs across the United States.

His bridge experience includes steel plate girder, prestressed concrete, continuous composite, culvert, substructure, and foundation systems. He has supported new bridge design, replacement and widening, rehabilitation, load rating, condition assessment, temporary works, shop drawing review, and construction-phase problem solving.

Peng's representative prior project experience includes the Hampton Roads Bridge-Tunnel Expansion in Virginia; the MTA/LIRR East Side Access program and Gowanus Expressway rehabilitation in New York City; multi-span VDOT bridge replacements; curved, skewed, and railroad steel-girder bridges in Ohio; NJDOT bridge and culvert projects; and structural inspection programs at major Port Authority aviation facilities.

Having served as a structural designer, construction engineer, consulting engineer, and owner's representative, Peng understands the priorities of designers, contractors, reviewers, and owners. He evaluates each assignment from a full-lifecycle perspective — considering not only calculation and code compliance, but also constructability, sequencing, inspection access, durability, maintenance, and clear documentation.

Peng earned a Master of Science in Civil Engineering from Rutgers University and a Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering from Xi'an University of Architecture & Technology. He is a licensed Professional Engineer in Virginia, Maryland, Washington, D.C., New York, and New Jersey.
